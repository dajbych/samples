Star Trek Online's Upgrade Weekend Event

StarTrek.com Staff

January 01, 2016

Tweet
Share
Comments (0)
A+ A A-

Still striving to get the greatest performance out of your equipment? This weekend will be the perfect time to apply Tech Upgrades to your gear, as Star Trek Online is doubling the amount of Technology Points earned from each one applied.

From now until January 4th at 10 a.m. PST, every Tech Upgrade used will apply twice as many Technology Points toward your next Upgrade. This, in turn, reduces the amount of Dilithium that must be spent to reach each Upgrade threshold, allowing Captains additional chances to attain both Mark and Rarity increases on their favored load outs.

Joining the fun at Star Trek Online is easy and free. Just visit startrekonline.com, register for a free account and then download and install the game. Once you've done that, just log in with your new account and you're ready to discover the entire Star Trek Online universe.
