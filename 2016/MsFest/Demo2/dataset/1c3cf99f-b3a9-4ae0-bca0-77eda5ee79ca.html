The paint was very durable....and retained it's shine

very weathered Hellcats ... not at sea so showing paint chipping, and heavy weathering, not how well most of the paint has held up though.

I believe there was a non-specular (ie matt) blue in front of the cockpit for anti glare

also, corrosion control at sea means paint chips were touched up

so, pretty glossy and no paint chips...

This is the sort of thing I'd ask on Hyperscale Plane Talking, as they have a much larger US readership, and more knowledge on this kind of thing.
