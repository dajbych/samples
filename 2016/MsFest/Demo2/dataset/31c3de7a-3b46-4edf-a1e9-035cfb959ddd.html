Gambling in Chicago

The Chicago mayor’s office warmly greeted passage of a bill did would give the Windy City a casino. So did some of the commenters on our stories about the passage on chicagotribune.com.

But there was a lively volley of viewpoints online from our readers; to say gambling would be universally welcomed in Chicago is an overstatement.

Many did not see the benefit and likened passage to the state lottery’s promise to fund education in Illinois. (Remember that?) A few Wondered how even the biggest infusion of cash Could overcome the budget holes faced by Chicago and Illinois. One man Seemed on the verge of moving away.

On the other hand, many of our online readers look forward to the tourists and jobs. These votes are the people’s will, They Said, And They urge Gov. Quinn to get out of the way and sign.
